When you arrive at the airport, follow the customs procedures. If you have questions, message ISS or a friend for help. If you plan to take a taxi to campus, have at least 600 yuan (800 is better) in cash to pay the driver.

We have a few apps that we suggest you to download before arrival to make travel easier!

  • Most importantly, download WeChat! It's easier for communication in china. Once you get a chinese bank account, you'll be able to purchase things using it as well!
  • In order to make purchases, it might be easiest to download Alipay. It's also more friendly for international cards!

When it comes to actually getting to school, there are at least two ways to do it. You can either take a taxi or hop on the train. Always let someone know you're on the way!

  • Baidu Maps gives you a bunch of options of how to get to school.
  • DiDi (滴嗒出行) is another app that lets you order a taxi! Don't forget that you have to have Alipay in order to pay for the taxi!

No matter how you end up in Kunshan, you need an address to go to!

Once you pass through customs, head towards the taxi line or take the train to Kunshan or Kunshan South Station. If you are taking a taxi, show the driver one of these addresses:

  • 江苏省-苏州市-昆山市-博士路99号雅颂悦里a3号 which will take you to the entrance to TA's bus stop
  • or 江苏省-苏州市-昆山市-杜克大学西门(江苏省昆山中学南) which will drop you off at the west gate on campus, outside of CC (Conference Center Building)
