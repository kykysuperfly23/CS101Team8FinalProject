How to get a Chinese Bank Account

Your suggested deadline for this item is: {{ bankDL }}


This is a necessary asset to have as a foriegner in China since a lot of services don't accept international cards.

ISS recommends you get a China Construction Bank card (CCB) since this is the bank DKU works with. This is the branch they suggest you go to, specificially visiting the 2nd floor: 昆山市前进西路228号中国建设银行. They also advise that you contact ISS a day before so they can set up a meeting with the bank manager for you.

You will need a document that states your purpose for opening a bank account. One in English, and one in Chinese. You can copy these blank versions here, or you can contact the ISS worker Ruoxuan (WeChatID: H1317126892) to make you personalized copies and help you with your other bank needs.

This is a full list of the documents & items you'll need to bring:

  • Passport Information & Visa Page, Copy and Original
  • Admission Notice, Copy and Original
  • Your DKU ID Card, Copy and Original
  • Purpose of Statement, Chinese and English
  • Simple Resume
  • 24-HR Registration Form Copy
