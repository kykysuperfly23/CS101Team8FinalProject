You'll need a chinese phone number to not only access most chinese apps, but to get a chinese bank account.

China has three cell carriers, China Mobile, China Unicom, and China Telecom.

ISS suggests that you go to China Telecom, specifically the one at this address: 中国电信北门路手机卖场(高新区工维中心) 江苏省苏州市昆山市北门路818号2楼

Other DKU Students have suggested China Mobile, specifically the one at this address: 江苏省-苏州市-昆山市-玉山镇前进西路1390号
